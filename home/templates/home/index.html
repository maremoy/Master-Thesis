My Research

Cross-Cultural User Interfaces

Today many people are used to using different types of graphical user interfaces. Therefore, most people also has an idea about what a good graphical user interface is. However, is good user interfaces the same for every culture or does it vary? Earlier research has established that culture can affect what users see as user friendly and aesthetically beautiful. The objective of my research is to investigate this further and get a better idea about whether a person’s perception of how beautiful or user friendly a graphical user interface is depends on the cultural background of the person.

The Survey

Localized Web Shops

To conduct my research I need to collect data from users. To do this I made a survey about people's perception of beauty and user friendiness in different web shops. In the survey the participants will be asked to rate pictures of web sites according to how beautiful and user friendly the web participant think the web site is. Then there will be a few easy questions about the participants. The entire survey is estimated to take 15 min and it is anonymous. No traceable data will be stored.
